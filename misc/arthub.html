The ArtHub

Graphic Design, Branding

During the summer of 2016, I was interning as Graphic Designer/Jr. Art Director at Winged Canvas, a local art studio in the city of Markham. I was given the opportunity to lead and direct a new brand that is a part of Winged Canvas: The ArtHub. We wanted the brand to feel "artsy" as Winged Canvas is an art studio. We went with the direction of a photo-realistic logo. As a former fine arts student I spent many hours drawing and painting letters with many different styles. Framing and printing is a service Winged Canvas also offers so I incorporated that by creating a letter with sample frames we had. This project was made by using photography, Adobe Illustrator, and Adobe Photoshop.
