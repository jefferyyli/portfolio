The Network

Editorial Design, Graphic Design

During my senior year of highschool, I was editor and designer on the yearbook team. The theme for our yearbook was the network as our team believed that our generation is very well connected through technology. We wanted our designs to feel a sense of connection for our graduating year. I designed the front cover of the yearbook, title page, table of contents, and dividers.
